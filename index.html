Privacy Policy

JeaHari is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in connection with our apps.

1. Information We Collect

JeaHari apps collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services. The specific data collected may vary by app and is described in each app's in-app privacy notice.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate our services
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To respond to user support requests

3. Sharing of Information

JeaHari does not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by applicable law.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain collected information only as long as necessary to provide our services. Upon request or account deletion, personal data is removed promptly.

5. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at Jeaharichang@gmail.com.
